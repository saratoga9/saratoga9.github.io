  • Utah County Emergency Rental Assistance Program

    The Utah County Commission has reached out to make everyone aware of the Utah County Emergency Rental Assistance Programs. Our County has been allocated funds from the federal government specifically to assist individuals and families who are struggling to pay their rent and utilities. Information for this program and a linkto apply can be found at rentrelief.utah.gov
    Application assistance can be found by contacting the Community Action Services at (801)373-8200 or by walking in at the following address: 815 S Freedom Blvd #100, Provo, UT 84601
